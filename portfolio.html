EpicGames

Our website offers an interactive platform for both solo and team-based online games. Users can create fully personalized accounts, complete with unique gaming avatars. The platform features a wide variety of games, including poker, quizzes, car racing, Connect Four, drawing games, and many more. Once logged in, you can challenge friends or join matches with players from around the world — all while enjoying an immersive and engaging experience. This site is designed to bring gaming enthusiasts together through a user-friendly interface packed with rich features. Communication : Rest API & SocketIO Backend : Express.js Frontend : TypeScript/ReactJS Database : MariaDB

DashBoard

Our web project offers full customization of the homepage through a variety of interactive widgets. Users can configure widgets to monitor air quality by entering their city name, get local weather forecasts, and display Spotify playlists or favorite artists by connecting their account. Additionally, the platform allows users to translate text using Google Translate and track cryptocurrency prices by entering the name of the desired coin. This platform is designed to provide a personalized and intuitive user experience, tailored to each user's needs and interests. APis used : Météo France, Spotify, Google traduction, Binance Backend : Spring boot Frontend : React Database : MariaDB

Wask

Our innovative app, WASK (Who Ask?), is transforming the way you interact with friends — much like BeReal, but with a twist. Every day, you’ll receive a random notification inviting you to log in and choose your game mode: answer a question, snap a photo, or tell a truth. Once you've submitted your response, you'll unlock your friends' answers and try to guess who said what. WASK turns everyday interactions into spontaneous, playful moments of connection and discovery, strengthening your relationships in a fun and engaging way. Backend : Spring boot Frontend : React Database : Mysql

ChatTech
